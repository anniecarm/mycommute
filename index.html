MY COMMUTE:

DISTRUPTED BY THE L

By Annie Carmichael

My Plan:

On Tuesday the 5th of February, I had a plan to walk two dogs. Mia first, then Moe.

1:35pm

  1. Leave my apartment.
  2. Walk three blocks.
  3. Enter the Jefferson L station.
  4. See turnstyles blarring red "no entry signs."
  5. Begin panicking.
"Trains are stopped. We have no idea when more are coming." - subway station booth Agent

I check Google Maps to find another route. The nearest subway station is the M; a 15 minute walk from my location. From there, it's another 40 minutes to get to Mia once I get there. That is, if the M decides to show up on time.

Not Likely.

Next step: Make sure Mia gets a walk

1:44pm

  1. Start walking toward the M.
  2. Text Mia's dad.
  3. Call Wag and ask for advice.
  4. Wag finds a cover.
  5. Text Jeffrey.
  6. Breathe.

Now that Mia is all set, I have two hours on my hands before my next walk. I've been walking towards the M, and my friend Tara lives a few blocks away. We had planned for her to meet me for my second walk, so I decided to head to her place instead.

As I'm walking to Tara's I see a dead rat on the ground. I add it to collection of abandoned things instagram stories.

Once I get to Tara's we listen to music and have coffee for an hour and then head head out to go walk Moe

3:05pm

  1. Start walking toward the M.
  2. Tara realizes she left her wallet
  3. We walk back to get it.
  4. We walk back to the train.
  5. The detour takes 10 minutes.

3:16pm

  1. We wait for two minutes.
  2. We get on the M.
  3. Transfer to the J at Myrtle Ave
  4. Get off at Fulton Street Station

3:55pm

Tara and I walk 5 minutes to Moe's apartment. Click play below to listen to the sounds of the city during our walk.

4:00pm

  1. Get Moe from the apartment.
  2. Walk him to the dog park
  3. Watch him play with other dogs.
  4. Bring Moe home after 30 mins.

4:44pm

Tara and I head home and take the L which has only now resumed service.
